Our Approach

Accuracy and reliability by design

Built like institutional research - multi-layered verification at every step, not just at the end.

Multi-Source Triangulation

The system triangulates information from multiple independent sources, automatically refines searches when initial results are insufficient, and uses branching logic that breaks complex compliance questions into sub-questions to guarantee complete coverage.

Source-Level Document Verification

Every regulation is downloaded as an official PDF directly from the source. Computer vision verifies document authenticity and extracts visual information - technical diagrams, certification marks, and complex tables that traditional text extraction misses.

Zero Information Loss

Every byte of content is preserved through semantic segmentation with intentional overlaps, ensuring no critical information falls through the cracks between processing stages.

Complete Traceability

Every identified compliance requirement traces back to its exact source with page number and citation context. The system assigns confidence scores and explicitly documents reasoning for every decision.

Transparent Uncertainty Handling

When ambiguities arise, the system doesn't hide them. It transparently indicates the uncertainty level and automatically initiates follow-up research loops - exactly as a careful team of experts would double-check critical interpretations.

Built-In Quality Assurance

Automatic quality controls at every decision point - from initial document verification to final requirement mapping. A complete audit trail documents not only what was decided, but how and why.
